---
title: "Clinical evolutions"
description: "Recording and managing clinical evolution notes for patients"
---

A **clinical evolution** (`hc_evoluciones`) represents a single patient encounter note — one visit, one record. Each evolution captures the full narrative of a consultation using a SOAP-adjacent structure, from the patient's chief complaint through the attending physician's treatment plan.

Evolutions are stored in the `hc_evoluciones` Supabase table and are always scoped to a specific patient via `paciente_id`. They are returned in reverse chronological order (most recent first).

## Routes

| Method | Path | Description |
|--------|------|-------------|
| `GET` | `/hc/evoluciones/paciente/<paciente_id>` | List all evolutions for a patient |
| `GET` | `/hc/evoluciones/nuevo/<paciente_id>` | Render the new evolution form |
| `POST` | `/hc/evoluciones/crear/<paciente_id>` | Submit and create a new evolution |

## Fields

<ParamField path="paciente_id" type="integer" required>
  Foreign key linking the evolution to a specific patient record. Set automatically from the URL — cannot be changed after creation.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="motivo_consulta" type="string" required>
  **Chief complaint / reason for visit.** A concise statement of why the patient is presenting today, in the patient's own words where possible.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="enfermedad_actual" type="string" required>
  **History of current illness.** A detailed narrative describing the onset, progression, duration, and character of the presenting complaint.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="examen_fisico" type="string" required>
  **Physical examination findings.** Objective observations recorded by the clinician during the encounter, including relevant system findings.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="impresion_diagnostica" type="string" required>
  **Diagnostic impression.** The clinician's working diagnosis or differential diagnosis based on the history and physical examination.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="plan" type="string" required>
  **Treatment plan.** The proposed management — medications prescribed, referrals, follow-up instructions, and any further diagnostic workup.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="medico" type="string" required>
  **Attending physician name.** The full name of the clinician conducting and signing off on this encounter.
</ParamField>

<Tip>
  Keep **motivo_consulta** short and focused — it is the chief complaint in one or two sentences at most. Use **enfermedad_actual** for the full chronological narrative: when symptoms started, how they have evolved, associated symptoms, and relevant history.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Evolutions are permanently linked to the patient identified in the URL (`paciente_id`). There is no mechanism to reassign an evolution to a different patient after it has been created.
</Note>

## Recording a new evolution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the patient record">
    Navigate to `/hc/pacientes/ver/<id>` to open the patient's profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the evolutions list">
    Click **Evoluciones** to view all existing evolution notes for this patient at `/hc/evoluciones/paciente/<paciente_id>`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the new evolution form">
    Click **Nueva evolución** to open the blank consultation form at `/hc/evoluciones/nuevo/<paciente_id>`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in all fields">
    Complete all six fields: chief complaint, history of current illness, physical examination, diagnostic impression, treatment plan, and attending physician name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Submit the form. On success, the application displays the flash message **"Evolución registrada correctamente"** and redirects back to the evolutions list for that patient.
  </Step>
</Steps>
