---
title: "Headquarters (Sedes)"
description: "Managing healthcare facility locations in VITACORE"
---

Headquarters — called **sedes** in the system — represent the physical locations where healthcare services are delivered. Every office (consultorio) and professional assignment is tied to a sede.

**Route:** `/hc/configuracion/sedes`

## Fields

<ParamField body="codigo" type="string" required>
  A short, unique identifier for the sede (e.g. `SEDE001`, `NORTE`, `CLINICA-A`). The value is stored in uppercase. Used as a reference key across the system and must be globally unique.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="nombre" type="string" required>
  The display name of the sede as it appears in lists and selection dropdowns.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="ciudad" type="string">
  The city where this sede is located. Optional but recommended for multi-city facilities.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="direccion" type="string">
  Street address of the sede.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="telefono" type="string">
  Contact phone number for this location.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="estado" type="string">
  Status of the sede. Accepted values: `ACTIVA` or `INACTIVA`.
</ParamField>

## CRUD flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="List sedes">
    Navigate to `/hc/configuracion/sedes`. The list is ordered by status (active first) then by name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a sede">
    Click **Nueva sede** and fill in the form. `codigo` and `nombre` are required. The system rejects duplicate codes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit a sede">
    Click the edit action on any sede row. All fields are editable. The duplicate-code check excludes the current record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle status">
    Use the toggle action to switch a sede between `ACTIVA` and `INACTIVA` without deleting it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Status values

| Value | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `ACTIVA` | Sede is operational and visible in selection lists |
| `INACTIVA` | Sede is disabled; hidden from new assignments |

</Note>
