---
title: "Medical services"
description: "Managing the medical services catalog in VITACORE"
---

The services catalog defines the specific medical procedures and consultations that can be scheduled and billed within VITACORE. Each service can optionally be linked to a medical specialty.

**Route:** `/hc/configuracion/servicios`

## Fields

<ParamField body="codigo" type="string" required>
  A unique service code (e.g. `CONS-MG`, `ECO-OB`, `LAB-HGB`). Must be unique across all services.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="nombre" type="string" required>
  The display name of the service as it appears in scheduling and billing forms.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="especialidad_id" type="integer">
  Optional foreign key to `hc_especialidades`. Links the service to a medical specialty. When omitted the service is treated as general or cross-specialty.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="descripcion" type="string">
  Free-text description of the service. Optional.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="estado" type="string">
  Status of the service. Accepted values: `ACTIVO` or `INACTIVO`. Controlled via the toggle action.
</ParamField>

## CRUD flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="List services">
    Navigate to `/hc/configuracion/servicios`. Records are ordered alphabetically by name. The specialty name is shown as a joined column from `hc_especialidades`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a service">
    Navigate to `/hc/configuracion/servicios/nuevo`. Fill in `codigo` and `nombre`. Optionally select a specialty.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit a service">
    Navigate to `/hc/configuracion/servicios/<id>/editar` or use the edit action in the list. All fields are editable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle status">
    Use the toggle action to switch between `ACTIVO` and `INACTIVO` without deleting the record.
  </Step>
</Steps>
