## Recommended setup order

Follow this order to avoid dependency errors when configuring VITACORE for the first time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create document types">
    Document types are required when registering patients and healthcare professionals. Go to [Document types](/configuration/document-types) and add at least the identification documents used in your country (e.g. national ID, passport).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create specialties">
    Specialties are referenced by both healthcare professionals and services. Go to [Specialties](/configuration/specialties) and add all medical disciplines practiced at your facility.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create headquarters">
    Headquarters (sedes) represent the physical locations of your facility. Go to [Headquarters](/configuration/headquarters) and register at least one active sede before creating offices or assigning professionals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create offices">
    Offices (consultorios) belong to a sede. Go to [Offices](/configuration/offices) and add the individual consultation rooms. Each office requires a sede to exist first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register healthcare professionals">
    With document types, specialties, sedes, and offices in place you can register the clinical staff under **Configuration → Professionals**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create roles and assign permissions">
    Go to [Roles](/configuration/roles), create at least one role, and assign per-module permissions via the permission matrix.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create user accounts">
    Go to [Users](/configuration/users) and create login accounts for each staff member, linking them to a role.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Sample seed data for document types, specialties, and sedes is available as JSON files in the `data/` directory of the source repository. You can use these files to pre-populate the database during initial deployment.
</Tip>
